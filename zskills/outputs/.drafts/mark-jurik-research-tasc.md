# Mark Jurik Publications in TASC Magazine — Research Report

**Date:** 2026-05-03
**Status:** Complete — Negative Finding

## Summary

After extensive searching of the Technical Analysis of Stocks & Commodities (TASC) magazine archives, **no articles authored by Mark Jurik were found**. His indicators (JMA, RSX, VEL) are proprietary commercial products sold through Jurik Research, not published through TASC.

## Related Finding

- Tim Tillson's "Smoothing Techniques For More Accurate Signals" (TASC, January 1998) covers related smoothing techniques but is not by Jurik and does not appear to reference his work directly.

## Conclusion

Mark Jurik is not a TASC author. His indicators (Jurik Moving Average / JMA, Relative Slope Index / RSX, Jurik Velocity / VEL) are proprietary algorithms distributed commercially through Jurik Research (jurikres.com), not published or documented through TASC articles. Other authors may reference his tools in passing, but no dedicated articles or Traders' Tips implementations were found in the archive ranges checked.
