# Mark Jurik - Research Biography

## Biography

- **Founder** of Jurik Research & Consulting (later Jurik Research Software, Inc.), founded **1988** in Silicon Valley.
- Specializes in **data modeling and time series forecasting methods**.
- Background in **signal processing** — the company's "About" page states that "signal processing skills originally intended for military projects are now successfully applied to the commercial arena" (post-Cold War pivot).
- **Lecturer and instructor for over a decade** covering theoretical and practical aspects of neural network technology.
- Lectured at **28+ conferences and seminars** (documented list spans 1987–2001).
- Created **"NeuroTapes"**, a 12-hour video course on neural network technology that sold worldwide for more than a decade.
- Published articles in **Futures Magazine** and the **Journal of Computational Intelligence in Finance** (also referred to as "Neurovest journal" and "AI in Finance").
- Contributing author to the book **"Virtual Trading"**.
- Company address (as listed): 556 South Fair Oaks Avenue, #595, Pasadena, CA 91105.
- As of ~2022, the company is **winding down operations** after 34 years (1988–2022).

**Source:** http://jurikres.com/about/company.htm (verified reachable via HTTP, HTTPS has TLS cert issues)

## Company: Jurik Research & Consulting / Jurik Research Software, Inc.

### Products & Services

The company develops **advanced technical analysis algorithms** for financial trading platforms. Core products:

| Product | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **JMA** (Jurik Moving Average) | Low-lag, smooth moving average; flagship product |
| **VEL** | Noise-free market velocity/momentum indicator |
| **RSX** | Smooth version of RSI with no added lag |
| **DMX** | Improved Directional Movement Index |
| **CFB** | Composite Fractal Behavior indicator |
| **DDR** | Data decorrelation/dimension reduction tool |
| **WAV** | Wavelet-based temporal compression |

## Books

### 1. "Computerized Trading: Maximizing Day Trading and Overnight Profits" (1999)

- **Role:** Editor
- **Publisher:** New York Institute of Finance
- **Description:** Twenty authors provide expertise on system development and trading technique. Covers basic trading skills, testing/evaluation, market psychology, advanced indicators, and forecasting.
- **Notable contributors/chapters:** DiNapoli (Trading Methods), Kiev (Psychology of Trading), Klimasauskas (Data Preprocessing), Katz (Advanced Trading System), plus Jurik's own appendix chapters on data sources, books, consultants, and software.
