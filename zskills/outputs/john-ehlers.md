# John F. Ehlers

## Summary

John F. Ehlers is an American electrical engineer, aerospace veteran, and pioneering technical analyst who applies **digital signal processing (DSP)** theory to financial markets. He is the president of MESA Software and Chief Scientist of StockSpotter.com. Over a career spanning four decades in trading research, he has published four books, 80+ articles in *Technical Analysis of Stocks & Commodities* (TASC) magazine, and created dozens of indicators and filters that are now standard tools on major trading platforms. He received the **Charles H. Dow Award** from the Market Technicians Association [1].

## Biography

### Education

- **BSEE & MSEE** — University of Missouri [1]
- Doctoral studies at **The George Washington University** — Major: Fields & Waves; Minor: Information Theory [1]
- **Emeritus Member, Sigma Xi** (The Scientific Research Honor Society) [1]

### Aerospace & Defense Career (pre-trading)

- Enlisted in the **U.S. Air Force** during the Korean War (early 1950s) [1]
- Antenna engineer — designed antennas for the **B-52** [1]
- Systems Engineer for the **AN/SLD-1** (Navy's first shipborne antisubmarine Direction Finder) [1]
- Participated in **IFF system** design using Fourier Transform of radar echoes from jet engine fan blades [1]
- Designed electronic filters and the **data transmitter for SkyLab** [1]
- Entrepreneur: semiconductor-based gas leak detector — awarded **Top 100 Products of 1976** by Industrial Research Magazine [1]
- **Director of Engineering, Norlin Communications** — precision surveillance receivers [1]
- Joined **Raytheon in 1980**, retired in **2005** as **Senior Engineering Fellow** [1]
- Systems Engineer for: AN/ALQ-184 Radar Jamming Pod, AN/ALE-50 Towed Decoy, SR-71 Self-Protect Suite, MALD (Miniature Air Launched Decoy) [1]

### Trading Career

- In **1978**, adapted Maximum Entropy Spectrum Analysis (MESA) from seismic exploration to market cycle measurement [1]
- Founded **MESA Software** [1]
- **R-MESA** — rated top 10 S&P trading system for over 10 years by Futures Truth [1]
- Contributing Editor, *Stocks & Commodities* magazine — 75+ articles published [1]
- Holds **annual workshops** each October [1][4]
- **Chief Scientist, StockSpotter.com** [1]

### Location

- Cambria, California [1]
- President, Cambria Historical Society [1]
- Adjutant, American Legion Post 432 [1]

### Awards

- **Charles H. Dow Award** (Market Technicians Association) [1]
- **Charles H. Dow Award Runner-up (2008)** — for the paper "Inferring Trading Strategies from Measured Probability Density Functions" [5]
- **Stocks & Commodities 2015 Readers' Choice Award** [1]
- **Judge for NAAIM Wagner Awards** [1]
