# Blog Post Templates

12 structural templates with target word counts. Select based on content type and audience intent.

## Template Selection Guide

| If the post is about... | Use | Words |
|---|---|---|
| How to accomplish something step-by-step | How-To Guide | 2,000-2,500 |
| A ranked or numbered collection | Listicle | 1,500-2,000 |
| Real-world results with metrics | Case Study | 1,500-2,000 |
| Comparing two or more options | Comparison | 1,500-2,000 |
| Comprehensive authority on a broad topic | Pillar Page | 3,000-4,000 |
| First-hand assessment of a tool/product | Product Review | 1,500-2,000 |
| An opinion or contrarian analysis | Thought Leadership | 1,500-2,500 |
| Expert quotes or curated resources | Roundup | 1,500-2,000 |
| Code/tool walkthrough | Tutorial | 2,000-3,000 |
| A timely event or announcement | News Analysis | 800-1,200 |
| Original data or research findings | Data Research | 2,000-3,000 |
| Answering many related questions | FAQ / Knowledge Base | 1,500-2,000 |

## How-To Guide

```
# How to [Achieve Specific Outcome]

> Key Takeaways (3-5 bullets)

## Why [This Approach] Works
[40-60 word answer-first paragraph]
[Context: when to use, prerequisites]

## Step 1: [Action Verb + Specific Task]
[What to do, why, expected result]

## Step 2: [Action Verb + Specific Task]
...

## Step N: [Action Verb + Specific Task]
...

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
[2-3 pitfalls with fixes]

## Next Steps
[What to do after completing the guide]
```

## Listicle

```
# [Number] [Category] [Qualifier] in [Year/Context]

> Key Takeaways

## 1. [Item Name]: [One-Line Value Prop]
[40-60 word summary]
[Evidence, example, or data point]

## 2. [Item Name]: [One-Line Value Prop]
...

## How to Choose [the Right One]
[Decision framework or comparison table]
```

---

## Case Study

```
# How [Company/Team] [Achieved Specific Result]

> Key Takeaways (include the headline metric)

## The Challenge
[What problem they faced, with specifics]

## The Approach
[What they did, methodology, tools]

## The Results
[Numbers: before/after, timeline, scale]

## Key Lessons
[3-5 transferable insights]

## How to Apply This
[Reader's next steps]
```

## Comparison

```
# [Option A] vs [Option B]: [Decision Context]

> Key Takeaways (include the recommendation)

## Quick Comparison

| Feature | Option A | Option B |
|---------|----------|----------|
| ... | ... | ... |

## When to Choose [Option A]
[40-60 word answer, then supporting detail]

## When to Choose [Option B]
[40-60 word answer, then supporting detail]

## Head-to-Head: [Key Dimension 1]
## Head-to-Head: [Key Dimension 2]
## Head-to-Head: [Key Dimension 3]

## The Verdict
[Clear recommendation with context]
```

## Pillar Page

```
# The Complete Guide to [Topic]

> Key Takeaways

## What is [Topic]?
[Definition + why it matters — 40-60 words]

## [Subtopic 1]: [Claim]
[Deep section — links to supporting articles if they exist]

## [Subtopic 2]: [Claim]
...

## [Subtopic N]: [Claim]
...

## Frequently Asked Questions
[5-8 Q&A pairs, 40-60 word answers]

## Further Reading
[Links to related articles in the series]
```

## Thought Leadership

```
# [Contrarian Claim or Reframe]

> Key Takeaways

## The Conventional Wisdom
[What most people believe, stated fairly]

## Why That's Wrong (or Incomplete)
[Your argument with evidence]

## What I've Seen Instead
[Personal experience, data, or examples]

## The Implications
[What changes if your view is correct]

## Where I Might Be Wrong
[Honest limitations of your argument]
```

## Tutorial

```
# [Build/Create/Implement] [Specific Thing] with [Tool/Language]

> Key Takeaways
> Prerequisites: [list]

## What We're Building
[Screenshot/diagram of end result]

## Setup
[Environment, dependencies, configuration]

## Step 1: [Component/Phase]
[Code + explanation]

## Step 2: [Component/Phase]
...

## Testing It
[How to verify it works]

## Extending It
[Next steps, variations, exercises]

## Full Code
[Complete working example or repo link]
```

## Data Research

```
# [Main Finding in Plain Language]

> Key Takeaways (lead with the number)

## The Question
[What we wanted to find out and why it matters]

## Methodology
[Data source, timeframe, analysis approach]

## Finding 1: [Specific Claim with Number]
[Evidence, visualization]

## Finding 2: [Specific Claim with Number]
...

## What This Means
[Implications for the reader]

## Limitations
[What this data can't tell us]

## Raw Data
[Link or appendix if appropriate]
```
