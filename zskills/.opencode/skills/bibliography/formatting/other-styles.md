# Other Citation Styles

Alternative styles to APA 7. Use when the target publication or audience requires a specific format.

---

## Nature Style

Used by Nature family journals and many natural sciences publications.

### In-Text
Superscript numbers in order of first appearance: `Recent studies^1,2^ have shown...`

### Reference List (numbered, by order of appearance)

**Journal article**:
```
1. Smith, J. D., Johnson, M. L. & Williams, K. R. Machine learning approaches in drug discovery. Nat. Rev. Drug Discov. 22, 301–318 (2023).
```

**Book**:
```
2. Kumar, V., Abbas, A. K. & Aster, J. C. Robbins and Cotran Pathologic Basis of Disease 10th edn (Elsevier, 2021).
```

**Preprint**:
```
3. Zhang, Y. et al. Novel therapeutic targets. Preprint at https://doi.org/10.1101/2024.01.001 (2024).
```

### Rules
- No "and" — use `&` between last two authors
- 1–5 authors: list all. 6+: first author et al.
- Journal names abbreviated (use standard ISSN abbreviations)
- Volume in bold: **22**
- Year in parentheses at end
- No DOI URL for journal articles (just the journal reference); include for preprints

## Vancouver Style

Used in biomedical and clinical journals. Based on ICMJE recommendations.

### In-Text
Superscript numbers in order of first appearance: `Recent studies^1,2^ have shown...`

### Reference List (numbered, by order of appearance)

**Journal article**:
```
1. Smith JD, Johnson ML, Williams KR. Machine learning approaches in drug discovery. Nat Rev Drug Discov. 2023;22(4):301-18.
```

**Book**:
```
2. Kumar V, Abbas AK, Aster JC. Robbins and Cotran pathologic basis of disease. 10th ed. Philadelphia: Elsevier; 2021.
```

**Website**:
```
3. Jurik M. How it works [Internet]. Jurik Research; 1999 [cited 2025 Apr 30]. Available from: http://jurikres.com/faq1/faq_ama.htm
```

### Rules
- No periods after initials: `Smith JD` not `Smith, J. D.`
- No "and" or "&" — just commas between all authors
- 1–6 authors: list all. 7+: list first 6, then "et al."
- Journal names abbreviated without periods
- Semicolon between year and volume: `2023;22(4):301-18`
- Hyphen (not en-dash) for page ranges

## IEEE Style

Used in engineering, computer science, and electrical/electronics publications.

### In-Text
Bracketed numbers in order of first appearance: `Recent studies [1], [2] have shown...`

### Reference List (numbered, by order of appearance)

**Journal article**:
```
[1] J. D. Smith, M. L. Johnson, and K. R. Williams, "Machine learning approaches in drug discovery," Nat. Rev. Drug Discov., vol. 22, no. 4, pp. 301–318, Apr. 2023.
```

**Book**:
```
[2] V. Kumar, A. K. Abbas, and J. C. Aster, Robbins and Cotran Pathologic Basis of Disease, 10th ed. Philadelphia, PA: Elsevier, 2021.
```

**Conference paper**:
```
[3] A. Vaswani et al., "Attention is all you need," in Proc. NeurIPS, Long Beach, CA, 2017, pp. 5998–6008.
```

**Website**:
```
[4] M. Jurik, "How it works," Jurik Research, 1999. [Online]. Available: http://jurikres.com/faq1/faq_ama.htm. [Accessed: Apr. 30, 2025].
```

### Rules
- First initial before last name: `J. D. Smith`
- Titles in quotes (articles, papers); italics for books/journals
- "and" before last author (not "&")
- 1–6 authors: list all. 7+: first author "et al."
- Abbreviated journal names
- Month abbreviated to 3 letters: Jan., Feb., Mar., etc.
- Volume as `vol.`, number as `no.`, pages as `pp.`

## Chicago Style (Author-Date)

Used in humanities, social sciences, history. Two variants exist; this covers Author-Date (sciences/social sciences).

### In-Text
- (Smith 2023)
- (Smith 2023, 45) — with page
- (Smith and Jones 2023)
- (Smith et al. 2023) — 3+ authors
- Smith (2023) argues...

### Reference List (alphabetical by author)

**Journal article**:
```
Smith, John D., Mary L. Johnson, and Karen R. Williams. 2023. "Machine Learning Approaches in Drug Discovery." Nature Reviews Drug Discovery 22 (4): 301–318. https://doi.org/10.1038/nrd.2023.001.
```

**Book**:
```
Kumar, Vinay, Abul K. Abbas, and Jon C. Aster. 2021. Robbins and Cotran Pathologic Basis of Disease. 10th ed. Philadelphia: Elsevier.
```

**Website**:
```
Jurik, Mark. 1999. "How It Works." Jurik Research. Accessed April 30, 2025. http://jurikres.com/faq1/faq_ama.htm.
```

### Rules
- Full first names (not just initials)
- Year immediately after author names
- Article titles in quotes; book/journal titles in italics
- No "and" — spell it out
- Alphabetical ordering
- Period after DOI/URL

## Quick Comparison

| Feature | APA 7 | Nature | Vancouver | IEEE | Chicago |
|---------|-------|--------|-----------|------|---------|
| In-text | (Author, Year) | ^number^ | ^number^ | [number] | (Author Year) |
| Order | Alphabetical | Appearance | Appearance | Appearance | Alphabetical |
| Author format | Last, F. M. | Last, F. M. | Last FM | F. M. Last | Last, First M. |
| "And" | & | & | comma only | and | and |
| Title case | Sentence | Sentence | Sentence | Sentence | Title Case (Chicago) |
| Journal italic | Yes | Yes | No | Yes | Yes |
