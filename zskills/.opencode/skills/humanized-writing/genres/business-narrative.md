# Business Narrative

For executive summaries, presentations, internal comms, launch posts, investor updates, postmortems.

## Story Structures

### SCR (Situation → Complication → Resolution)
Best for: executive summaries, status updates, problem-solving narratives.
- **Situation:** Establish the status quo (1-2 sentences)
- **Complication:** What changed, what's at risk
- **Resolution:** What we did / recommend / need

### Problem → Solution → Benefit
Best for: product launches, feature announcements, sales materials.
- **Problem:** Pain the audience feels (specific, named)
- **Solution:** What we built / changed
- **Benefit:** Measurable outcome for them

### Hero's Journey (simplified)
Best for: case studies, founder stories, transformation narratives.
- Ordinary world → Challenge → Struggle → Discovery → Transformation → New normal

### Before → After → Bridge
Best for: change management, process improvements.
- **Before:** How things were (with specific friction)
- **After:** How things are now (with specific improvement)
- **Bridge:** What we did to get there

## Audience Adaptation

| Audience | Priorities | Adjust |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| Executives | Time, decisions, risk | Lead with recommendation. Numbers in first paragraph. |
| Engineers | How it works, tradeoffs | Technical detail welcome. Show the system. |
| Investors | Growth, market, defensibility | Metrics first. Context second. Vision last. |
| Customers | Their problem, their outcome | You/your language. Benefits before features. |

## Rules

1. **One message per communication.** If you can't state it in one sentence, you have two communications.
2. **Numbers in the first paragraph.** Revenue, users, percentage, timeline — something concrete immediately.
3. **No throat-clearing.** Delete the first paragraph of most business writing; it's usually warm-up.
4. **Specific over impressive.** "Reduced deploy time from 45 minutes to 3 minutes" beats "dramatically accelerated our deployment pipeline."
5. **Name the tradeoff.** Every decision has a cost. Stating it builds trust.
